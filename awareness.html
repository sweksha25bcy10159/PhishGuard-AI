CYBER DEFENSE STRATEGY

Before you use our AI, understand the basics of Phishing defense.

✅ DO'S

  • Always check the sender's full email address.
  • Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
  • Look for "https://" and the padlock icon.
  • Report suspicious links to authorities.

❌ DON'TS

  • Don't click on links from unknown SMS or Emails.
  • Don't share OTPs or Passwords with anyone.
  • Don't trust "Urgent" or "Threatening" messages.
  • Don't download attachments from unverified sources.
